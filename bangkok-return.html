Bangkok Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 22, 2024 – Arrival & Check-in

2:00 PM – Check-in at Prince Palace Hotel

Address: 388/1 Ratchawithi Rd, Dusit, Bangkok.

Distance: ~30 km from Don Mueang International Airport (DMK) (30-40 minute taxi ride).

12:30 PM – Lunch Nearby

Enjoy lunch at a nearby restaurant.

Cost: ~200-300 THB for two.

Distance: ~1 km from Prince Palace Hotel (5-minute walk).

3:30 PM – Chatuchak Weekend Market

Explore this famous market for shopping and local snacks.

Duration: ~2-3 hours.

Cost: Free to enter (shopping cost varies).

Distance: ~4 km from the nearby restaurant (15-minute taxi ride).

6:00 PM – Wat Benchamabophit (Marble Temple)

Visit one of Bangkok's most beautiful temples.

Duration: ~1 hour.

Cost: ~50 THB per person.

Distance: ~3 km from Chatuchak Weekend Market (10-minute taxi ride).

7:30 PM – Return to Hotel

Take a taxi back to the Prince Palace Hotel.

Cost: ~100-150 THB.

Distance: ~3 km from Wat Benchamabophit (10-minute taxi ride).

Day 2: Nov 23, 2024 – Exploring Bangkok

8:00 AM – Breakfast at Hotel

Enjoy breakfast at Prince Palace Hotel.

Cost: ~300 THB for two.

Duration: ~1 hour.

10:00 AM – Pak Khlong Talat (Flower Market)

Visit the vibrant flower market to see a variety of flowers.

Duration: ~1-2 hours.

Cost: Free to enter (shopping cost varies).

Transportation: Taxi (~15 minutes, ~80-120 THB).

12:00 PM – Talad Rom Hub

Experience this unique market with various local foods and souvenirs.

Duration: ~2 hours.

Cost: Free to enter (shopping cost varies).

Transportation: Taxi (~30 minutes, ~100-150 THB).

2:30 PM – Lunch at Local Restaurant

Enjoy a delicious meal at a nearby restaurant.

Cost: ~600 THB for two.

Duration: ~1 hour.

4:00 PM – Assumption Cathedral

Visit this beautiful cathedral known for its architecture.

Duration: ~1 hour.

Cost: Free to enter.

Transportation: Taxi (~20 minutes, ~80-120 THB).

5:30 PM – Explore Khao San Road

Wander around Khao San Road, famous for its vibrant atmosphere.

Duration: ~1-2 hours.

Cost: Free to explore (shopping and food costs vary).

Transportation: Taxi (~15 minutes, ~80-120 THB).

7:30 PM – Dinner at a Rooftop Restaurant

Enjoy a scenic dinner with a view of the city skyline.

Cost: ~1,200 THB for two.

Duration: ~2 hours.

Transportation: Taxi (~15 minutes, ~80-120 THB).

9:30 PM – Return to Hotel

Take a taxi back to Prince Palace Hotel.

Cost: ~100-150 THB.

Day 3: Nov 24, 2024 – Departure

8:00 AM – Breakfast at Hotel

Enjoy breakfast at Prince Palace Hotel.

Cost: ~300 THB for two.

10:00 AM – Last-Minute Shopping

Explore nearby shopping areas or relax at the hotel.

Duration: ~2 hours.

Cost: Free (shopping cost varies).

12:30 PM – Lunch Nearby

Enjoy lunch at a nearby restaurant.

Cost: ~200-300 THB for two.

2:00 PM – Check Out and Depart for DMK Airport

Check out of the hotel and depart for DMK Airport for your flight.

Public Bus: You can take the A4 bus to DMK Airport.
Schedule: First bus at ~6:00 AM, last bus at ~8:00 PM.
Travel Time: Approximately 30-60 minutes depending on traffic.
Cost: ~50-60 THB per person.

Taxi: Alternatively, you can take a taxi to DMK Airport.
Cost: ~200-300 THB.

Keep your expenses low while enjoying your day:

  • Use Public Transport: Opt for songthaews (shared taxis) or motorbike taxis for affordable and local transport options.
  • Eat Like a Local: Visit local eateries or night markets for delicious and budget-friendly meals.
  • Free Attractions: Include free attractions like Wat Phra Yai to balance your costs.
  • Combine Destinations: Choose nearby attractions to minimize transportation costs.
