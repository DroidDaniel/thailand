Bangkok Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 16, 2024 – Exploring Bangkok

4:00 AM – Arrival at DMK Airport

After immigration, head to D&D Inn, Khaosan Road.

  • Distance: ~30 km
  • Transportation: Taxi (400-500 THB, 40-50 mins) or Airport Bus A4 (50 THB/person, ~60 mins)

Immigration and Visa Process

After landing, follow signs to the immigration area.

Time Required: 30-60 mins

Collecting a New SIM Card

Book and collect a SIM card from vendors inside the airport.

Cost: 300-500 THB

10:00 AM – Relax & Breakfast at Khaosan Road

Explore street food stalls (~200 THB for two).

Alternative: Breakfast at 7-Eleven

  • Location: 150 meters from hotel (~2 min walk)
  • Cost for Two: ~120-160 THB

12:00 PM – Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew

  • Distance: ~2 km from hotel (~20 mins walk)
  • Entry Fee: 500 THB/person
  • Spend 2-3 hours exploring.

2:30 PM – Wat Pho (Temple of Reclining Buddha)

  • Distance: ~650 meters (~10 mins walk)
  • Entry Fee: 200 THB/person
  • Spend 1 hour exploring.

3:30 PM – Riverside Snack at Tha Maharaj Mall

  • Distance: ~700 meters (~10 mins walk)
  • Cost: ~200 THB for two
  • Relax with scenic river views for 30 minutes.

4:30 PM – Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn)

  • Take a ferry from Tha Tien Pier (~5 mins, 4 THB).
  • Entry Fee: 100 THB/person

5:30 PM – Pak Khlong Talad (Flower Market)

  • Distance: Ferry back to Tha Tien Pier (~5 mins) + 1.5 km walk (~20 mins)
  • Spend 30 minutes exploring vibrant flower stalls.

6:30 PM – ICONSIAM Mall

  • Distance: Ferry to ICONSIAM (~10 mins)
  • Explore luxury shops, dining options, and river views.
  • Ideal Time: 1-2 hours

8:30 PM – Evening Rest at Hotel

Take a tuk-tuk (~50 THB) or ferry back to Khaosan Road for rest.

9:30 PM – Nightlife at Khaosan Road

Explore bars, street food, and live music (Budget: 700-1,000 THB).

11:00 PM – Back to Hotel & Rest

Day 2: Nov 17, 2024 – Half-Day in Bangkok & Travel to Jomtien Beach

8:00 AM – Breakfast at the Hotel

Enjoy breakfast at D&D Inn or explore nearby cafes.
Cost: ~150 THB for two.

9:30 AM – Visit Wat Saket (Golden Mount)

  • Distance: ~1.5 km from the hotel (~20 mins walk)
  • Entry Fee: 50 THB/person
  • Time: ~1 hour to explore

12:00 PM – Return to Hotel & Check-out

Check out from D&D Inn by 12 PM and prepare to head to the Ekkamai Bus Terminal for your trip to Pattaya.

12:30 PM – Travel to Ekkamai Bus Terminal via Grab

  • How to Get There: Use Grab for a direct, hassle-free ride from the hotel to the bus terminal.
  • Cost: ~200 THB
  • Time: ~30 mins (depending on traffic)
  • At Ekkamai Bus Terminal, buy tickets for the next available bus to Jomtien Beach, Pattaya.

1:30 PM – Bus to Jomtien Beach, Pattaya

  • Duration: ~2-2.5 hours
  • Cost: ~300-500 THB for two (depends on the bus type)

4:00 PM – Check-in at Happy Vikings Beachfront

  • Address: Jomtien Beach, Pattaya
  • Relax at the hotel and enjoy beachfront views.

5:30 PM – Evening Stroll along Jomtien Beach

Walk along the beach, enjoy the sunset, and explore local street food stalls.
Cost: ~300 THB for snacks and drinks.

7:30 PM – Dinner at a Beachfront Restaurant

  • Enjoy seafood or Thai cuisine with ocean views.
  • Cost: ~600-800 THB for two.

9:00 PM – Explore Nightlife or Relax

Optionally explore nightlife spots around Jomtien Beach or head back to the hotel for a relaxed evening.

To keep your expenses low while enjoying your day:

  • Use Public Transport: Opt for songthaews (shared taxis) or motorbike taxis for affordable and local transport options.
  • Eat Like a Local: Visit local eateries or night markets for delicious and budget-friendly meals.
  • Free Attractions: Include free attractions like Wat Phra Yai to balance your costs.
  • Combine Destinations: Choose nearby attractions to minimize transportation costs.
