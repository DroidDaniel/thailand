Bangkok Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 16, 2024 – Exploring Bangkok

4:00 AM – Arrival at DMK Airport

After immigration, head to D&D Inn, Khaosan Road.

  • Distance: ~30 km
  • Transportation: Taxi (400-500 THB, 40-50 mins) or Airport Bus A4 (50 THB/person, ~60 mins)

Immigration and Visa Process

After landing, follow signs to the immigration area. Time Required: Approximately 30-60 minutes.

Collecting a New SIM Card

You can book and collect your SIM card from vendors inside the DMK Airport after clearing immigration. Cost: Around 300-500 THB for a prepaid SIM.

Transportation to the City

  • Location: A4 bus terminal is on the ground floor outside arrivals.
  • Cost: 50 THB/person.

10:00 AM – Relax & Breakfast at Khaosan Road

Explore nearby street food stalls and cafes for breakfast (~200 THB for two).

Alternative: Breakfast at 7-Eleven

  • Location: 150 meters from your hotel (~2 min walk)
  • Cost for Two: ~120-160 THB

Recommended items for a quick bite:

  • Ham & Cheese Toasties – 25-30 THB
  • Rice Porridge (Jok) – 30-35 THB
  • Onigiri (Rice Balls) – 30-40 THB
  • Yogurt & Fruit Cups – 20-40 THB
  • Thai Iced Coffee or Milk Tea – 25-35 THB

12:00 PM – Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew

  • Distance: ~2 km from your hotel (walkable, ~20 mins)
  • Entry Fee: 500 THB/person
  • Spend 2-3 hours exploring this iconic landmark.

3:00 PM – Lunch at a Local Thai Restaurant

To get to a nearby lunch spot, walk approximately 1 km (15 mins) from the Grand Palace. A recommended option is:

Riverside Cafe

  • Distance: ~1 km from Grand Palace
  • Cost: ~400 THB for two.

Recommended dishes:

  • Pad Thai – 100-150 THB
  • Green Curry – 150-200 THB
  • Spring Rolls – 80-120 THB

4:30 PM – Visit Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn)

  • Distance: Take a ferry from Tha Tien Pier (~5 mins, 4 THB).
  • Entry Fee: 100 THB/person

6:00 PM – Evening Rest

To return to your hotel, take the ferry back to Tha Tien Pier and walk ~2 km or take a tuk-tuk for 50 THB.

8:00 PM – Nightlife at Khaosan Road

Explore bars, street food, and live music. Budget: 700-1,000 THB.

11:00 PM – Back to Hotel & Rest

Table of Costs for Day 1

Category Details Cost (THB)
Food Street food and local eateries ~400 THB
Transportation Public buses, ferries, and shared taxis ~300 THB
Site Entry Fees Grand Palace, Wat Arun ~600 THB
Nightlife and Extras Basic drinks and small souvenirs ~500 THB
Total Cost for Couple (Budget) Sum of all categories ~1,800 THB (~7,200 INR)

Day 2: Nov 17, 2024 – Half-Day in Bangkok & Travel to Jomtien Beach

8:00 AM – Breakfast at the Hotel

Enjoy breakfast at D&D Inn or explore nearby cafes.
Cost: ~150 THB for two.

9:30 AM – Visit Wat Saket (Golden Mount)

  • Distance: ~1.5 km from your hotel (~20 mins walk)
  • Entry Fee: 50 THB/person
  • Time: ~1 hour to explore

12:00 PM – Return to Hotel & Check-out

Check out from D&D Inn by 12 PM and head to the bus terminal for the trip to Pattaya.

12:30 PM – Travel to Ekkamai Bus Terminal

  • How to Get There: Take a BTS Skytrain from Khaosan Road to Ekkamai Station (via Siam Station interchange).
  • Cost: ~45 THB/person
  • Time: ~40 mins
  • At Ekkamai Station, buy tickets for the next available bus to Jomtien Beach.

1:30 PM – Bus to Jomtien Beach, Pattaya

  • Duration: ~2-2.5 hours
  • Cost: ~150-250 THB/person (~300-500 THB for two)

4:00 PM – Check-in at Happy Vikings Beachfront

  • Address: Jomtien Beach, Pattaya
  • Relax at the hotel and enjoy beachfront views.

5:30 PM – Evening Stroll along Jomtien Beach

Walk along the beach, enjoy the sunset, and explore local street food stalls.
Cost: ~300 THB for snacks and drinks.

7:30 PM – Dinner at a Beachfront Restaurant

  • Enjoy seafood or Thai cuisine with ocean views.
  • Cost: ~600-800 THB for two.

9:00 PM – Explore Nightlife or Relax

Optionally explore nightlife spots around Jomtien Beach or head back to the hotel for a relaxed evening.

Table of Costs for Day 2

Category Details Cost (THB)
Food Breakfast & snacks ~150 THB
Site Entry Fees Wat Saket (Golden Mount) ~100 THB
Transportation Skytrain & Bus ~550 THB (for two)
Food & Drinks Dinner at Jomtien Beach ~800 THB
Total Cost for Couple (Budget) Sum of all categories ~1,600 THB (~6,400 INR)
