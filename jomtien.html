Jomtien Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 17, 2024 – Arrival & Exploration

12:30 PM – Bus to Jomtien Beach

  • Duration: ~2-2.5 hours
  • Cost: ~300-500 THB for two
  • Distance: ~150 km from Bangkok
  • Mode of Transport: Bus
  • Walkable: No (bus required)
  • Alternative Transport: Minivan service (~400-600 THB for two)

3:00 PM – Check-in at Happy Vikings Beachfront

  • Address: Jomtien Beach, Pattaya
  • Enjoy beachfront views and relax after check-in.
  • Distance from Bus Stop: ~1 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Walk
  • Taxi Fare: ~100-150 THB
  • Walkable: Yes

4:30 PM – Visit Sanctuary of Truth

  • Duration: ~1.5-2 hours
  • Cost: ~1,000 THB for two
  • Distance from Hotel: ~4 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi
  • Taxi Fare: ~150-200 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Songthaew (local shared taxi) ~30 THB per person
  • Walkable: No (taxi recommended)

7:00 PM – Dinner at a Beachfront Restaurant

  • Enjoy fresh seafood or Thai cuisine with ocean views.
  • Cost: ~600-800 THB for two.
  • Distance from Sanctuary of Truth: ~4 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi
  • Taxi Fare: ~150-200 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Songthaew ~30 THB per person
  • Walkable: No (taxi recommended)

9:00 PM – Optional Nightlife Exploration

Explore local bars or head back to the hotel for a relaxing night.

  • Distance from Restaurant: ~1 km
  • Mode of Transport: Walk or Taxi
  • Walkable: Yes
  • Taxi Fare: ~50-100 THB if taking a taxi

Day 2: Nov 18, 2024 – Full Day of Attractions

8:00 AM – Breakfast at the Hotel

Enjoy breakfast at Happy Vikings Beachfront.

Cost: ~300 THB for two.

  • Distance from Hotel: Onsite
  • Mode of Transport: None
  • Walkable: Yes

9:30 AM – Visit Art in Paradise

Duration: ~2 hours

Cost: ~600 THB for two.

  • Distance from Hotel: ~5 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Grab
  • Taxi Fare: ~150-200 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Songthaew (shared taxi) for ~30-50 THB per person
  • Walkable: No

12:00 PM – Visit Wat Phra Yai (Big Buddha)

Duration: ~1 hour

Cost: Free (donations appreciated).

  • Distance from Art in Paradise: ~3 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Grab
  • Taxi Fare: ~100-150 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Motorbike taxi for ~60-80 THB per person
  • Walkable: No

1:30 PM – Lunch at a Local Restaurant

Enjoy a light lunch nearby.

Cost: ~500 THB for two.

  • Distance from Wat Phra Yai: ~1 km
  • Mode of Transport: Walk
  • Walkable: Yes

3:00 PM – Visit Mini Siam

Duration: ~1.5-2 hours

Cost: ~600 THB for two.

  • Distance from Lunch Spot: ~4 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Grab
  • Taxi Fare: ~150-200 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Songthaew (shared taxi) for ~30-50 THB per person
  • Walkable: No

5:30 PM – Visit Tiger Park Pattaya

Duration: ~1.5-2 hours

Cost: ~1,200 THB for two.

  • Distance from Mini Siam: ~6 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Grab
  • Taxi Fare: ~200-250 THB

  • Walkable: No

8:00 PM – Dinner at a Night Market

Explore local dishes at a vibrant night market.

Cost: ~500-700 THB for two.

  • Distance from Tiger Park: ~5 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or Grab
  • Taxi Fare: ~150-200 THB
  • Alternative Transport: Songthaew (shared taxi) for ~30-50 THB per person
  • Walkable: No

9:30 PM – Evening Beach Walk

Enjoy a peaceful walk along Jomtien Beach.

  • Distance from Night Market: ~3 km
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi or walk
  • Taxi Fare: ~100-150 THB
  • Walkable: Yes (recommended)

To keep your expenses low while enjoying your day:

  • Use Public Transport: Opt for songthaews (shared taxis) or motorbike taxis for affordable and local transport options.
  • Eat Like a Local: Visit local eateries or night markets for delicious and budget-friendly meals.
  • Free Attractions: Include free attractions like Wat Phra Yai to balance your costs.
  • Combine Destinations: Choose nearby attractions to minimize transportation costs.
