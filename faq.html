Frequently Asked Questions

What are the documents required for on-arrival visa for Indians? (How much does it cost?)
Indians need the following documents for an on-arrival visa in Thailand:
  • A passport valid for at least 30 days from the date of entry
  • A visa application form (available at the airport)
  • A recent photo (4x6 cm)
  • Confirmed return flight tickets within 15 days
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings or similar)
  • Proof of sufficient funds: 10,000 THB per person or 20,000 THB per family
The on-arrival visa fee is 2,000 THB (approximately ₹4,500), payable in Thai Baht.
How much money is needed for a 10-day trip? (Budget and expense)
For a budget trip, around ₹35,000 to ₹45,000 (15,000 to 20,000 THB) per person is sufficient, covering basic accommodations, food, and transport. For more comfort or luxury, it could cost ₹60,000 or more.
Is traveling by tuk-tuk safe?
Tuk-tuks are generally safe for tourists, but it's advisable to negotiate the fare in advance and avoid riding them too late at night in isolated areas.
What time does the entire city shut down?
Most shops and malls close by 9 PM or 10 PM, while bars and restaurants stay open till midnight or 2 AM. Some night markets operate until late at night.
Is Thailand a good destination for couples?
Yes, Thailand is a popular destination for couples due to its scenic beaches, islands, nightlife, and cultural attractions that offer romantic experiences.
How much money is needed for a single day?
A budget of 1,500 to 2,000 THB (₹3,500 to ₹4,500) per day is recommended for meals, transport, and sightseeing. For luxury activities, the daily expense can go higher.
What apps should I install for the trip?
  • Google Maps and Grab for navigation and transport
  • XE Currency for currency conversion
  • Klook for SIM booking, tours, and activities
Local buses and songthaews (shared taxis) are also available in major cities like Bangkok and Pattaya.
