Koh Larn Island Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 19, 2024 – Arrival & Exploration

12:00 PM – Check-out from Happy Vikings

Prepare for the transfer to Koh Larn Island.

1:00 PM – Ferry to Koh Larn

  • Duration: ~30 minutes
  • Cost: ~300 THB for two (round trip)

1:30 PM – Check-in at Lareena Resort

  • Address: Koh Larn, Pattaya
  • Enjoy beautiful beachfront views.

3:00 PM – Visit Tawaen Beach

Relax on the beach, swim, or try water sports.

Cost: Free (water sports additional).

6:00 PM – Dinner at a Beachfront Restaurant

Enjoy local cuisine while watching the sunset.

Cost: ~600-800 THB for two.

Day 2: Nov 20, 2024 – Activities & Relaxation

8:00 AM – Breakfast at Lareena Resort

Enjoy breakfast with a view of the beach.

Cost: ~300 THB for two.

10:00 AM – Snorkeling at Coral Beach

Explore the underwater beauty of Coral Beach.

Cost: ~800 THB for equipment rental.

1:00 PM – Lunch at a Local Cafe

Enjoy a light lunch at a beachside cafe.

Cost: ~500 THB for two.

3:00 PM – Relax & Sunbathe at the Resort

Unwind and enjoy the beach at Lareena Resort.

6:00 PM – Dinner at a Local Restaurant

Enjoy fresh seafood and local dishes.

Cost: ~600-800 THB for two.
