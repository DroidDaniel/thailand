Pattaya Itinerary

Day 1: Nov 21, 2024 – Arrival & Nong Nooch Botanical Garden

12:00 PM – Check-out from Lareena Resort, Koh Larn

Distance: ~2 km to the ferry pier (5-minute taxi or 10-minute walk).

1:00 PM – Ferry back to Pattaya

  • Duration: ~30 minutes
  • Cost: ~30-40 THB for single person
  • Distance: ~7 km from Koh Larn to Pattaya (ferry distance).

2:00 PM – Check-in at Balihai Bay Residence

Relax and freshen up after your journey.

Distance: ~5 km from the ferry pier to the hotel (15-minute taxi ride).

2:30 PM – Visit Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden

  • Explore the beautiful gardens and cultural shows.
  • Cost: ~600 THB for two (entrance fee)
  • Duration: ~3 hours
  • Transportation: Taxi or local transport from your hotel
  • Travel Duration: ~30 minutes
  • Distance: ~20 km from Balihai Bay Residence to Nong Nooch Garden (30-minute taxi ride).
  • Taxi Cost: ~300-400 THB for a one-way trip.
  • Alternative Transport: Rent a motorbike (if comfortable) or use a ride-hailing app like Grab.
  • Bus Option: Take a local bus from Pattaya to Nong Nooch Garden.
    • Cost: ~50 THB per person
    • Duration: ~45-60 minutes
    • Bus Station: Board from Pattaya Bus Station.

5:30 PM – Return to the hotel

Transportation: Taxi or local transport

Duration: ~15 minutes

Distance: ~20 km from Nong Nooch Garden back to the hotel (30-minute taxi ride).

7:00 PM – Dinner at a local seafood restaurant

Experience fresh seafood with ocean views.

Cost: ~800 THB for two.

Duration: ~1.5 hours

Distance: ~2 km from Balihai Bay Residence (5-minute taxi ride).

8:30 PM – Explore Walking Street

Enjoy the vibrant nightlife, shops, and street performances.

Cost: Free to explore (expenses vary based on activities).

Duration: ~2 hours

Distance: ~1 km from the seafood restaurant (10-minute walk).

How to Travel from Pattaya to Bangkok

  • Option 1: Bus from **North Pattaya Bus Station** to Bangkok (Ekkamai or Mo Chit Terminal)
    • Station Location: North Pattaya, accessible via taxi or songthaew (~10-15 minutes, ~80-100 THB)
    • Bus Duration: ~2-2.5 hours
    • Bus Cost: ~120-200 THB per person
  • Option 2: Hire a private taxi or use a ride-hailing app (like Grab)
    • Duration: ~1.5-2 hours (depending on traffic)
    • Cost: ~1,500-2,000 THB
  • From Bangkok Bus Terminal to Prince Palace Hotel:
    • Duration: ~15-30 minutes by taxi
    • Cost: ~100-200 THB

To keep your expenses low while enjoying your day:

  • Use Public Transport: Opt for songthaews (shared taxis) or motorbike taxis for affordable and local transport options.
  • Eat Like a Local: Visit local eateries or night markets for delicious and budget-friendly meals.
  • Free Attractions: Include free attractions like Wat Phra Yai to balance your costs.
  • Combine Destinations: Choose nearby attractions to minimize transportation costs.
